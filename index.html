What We Do With Your Information

We take your information on the surveys and enter them into a JSON
file where many people can surf through the data using a variety of
programming languages!

Types Of Data You're Giving Away

You are only giving away the data you enter into the survey.
You will not be giving away things like your email, your password
or your IP address.

Become A Pateron

If you would like to become a Pateron and support our website
simply click the button that says "Pateron" at the bottom of your screen!
We greatly appreciate any paterons no matter the tier! If you choose
to be a Pateron, you are funding our website for years to come!

Signing Up

If you would like to sign up, fill out our form to the
right of the page! From there, you can start filling out surveys.
